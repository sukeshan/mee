Food Recipe Retrieval

Introduction

Most of the time the user doesnâ€™t have an ingredient of SE-recommended recipes. To address this problem I created a search engine that will retrieve food recipes based on the user-given food ingredients instead of user-given food recipes.

Dataset

We scraped data from online websites such as Times of India and All recipes. We scraped recipe title, ingredients, cuisine, category, instructions, recipe image, ratings and webpage link.

Model

We tried two methodologies for this problem:

  • Embedding-based technique: We created embeddings for each ingredient by recipe instructions. To represent recipes in embedding space, we simply average the embeddings of each recipeâ€™s ingredient.
  • Graph-based technique: Using bipartite graph retrieve recipes simply and effectively. Nodes {RA, RB, RC, RD} belong to Recipes set and nodes {IA, IB, IC, ID} belong to Ingredients set.
